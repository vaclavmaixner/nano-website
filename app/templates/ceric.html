CERIC

We are a Partner Facility of the Central European Research Infrastructure Consortium (CERIC), which is one of the ERICs. Charles University is the Representing Entity of the Czech Republic to CERIC. We operate four instruments available for Open Access by researchers from all over the world. Three of these instruments are located in our Surface Physics Laboratory in Prague, the remaining one being a beamline at the Elettra synchrotron in Trieste (Italy). You can apply for measurement time on the instruments in regular calls for open access proposals, which are issued by CERIC twice a year.
