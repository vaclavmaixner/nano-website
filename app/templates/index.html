Nanomaterials Group

We are a research group at the Department of Surface and Plasma Science of the Faculty of Mathematics and Physics of the Charles University. We work in three main areas: basic research in nanomaterials, offering open access to several scientific instruments through CERIC, applied research in fuel cell technology. We are among the Large Research Infrastructures supported by the Czech Ministry of Education, Youth and Sports (grants SPL-MSB and OP VVV SPL-MSB).
