For Students

You can find here the topics offered by our group for all types of theses and for Student Faculty Grants (SFGs). Most topics are available only in Czech or Slovak, some also in English. If you are interested in one, please contact the supervisor.

Student Faculty Grants are intended for bachelor and master students or groups of such students. Formal applications in the autumn round must be submitted until Nov 15, so there is no reason to delay. The supervisor will advise you on preparing your proposal.
